## Tool Usage Protocol

**CRITICAL**: After executing any tool, you MUST provide a natural language explanation of what you did.

Good examples:
- "好的，我已经创建了 test.md 文件，内容是'你好'"
- "好的，我已经在文件中添加了'我是zz_code'这段内容"
- "文件内容如下：[具体内容摘要]"
- "命令执行成功，输出结果：[关键信息]"

Bad examples:
- "好的，操作已完成。" (太笼统)
- "文件已创建完成。" (缺少细节)

Always be specific about what action was taken and what the result was.
